Kobe Bryant

The Black Mamba

Kobe Bryant sits with his daughter Gianna Bryant at a WNBA game

Here is a timeline of events of the life of Kobe Bryant

  • 1978: Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996: Becomes the top highschool basketball player in the country and skips college to enter the NBA draft
  • 1999-2002: Wins three NBA championships in a row with the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2006: Scored his career high of 81 points which is the second most points scored in a game in NBA history
  • 2009-10: Wins his final two NBA championships with the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2016: Scores 60 points in his final NBA game before retiring and became the oldest player in NBA history to score 60 points in a game.
  • 2020: Kobe tragically passes away with his daughter, Gianna, and seven other people in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California

Kobe Bryant was one of the greatest basketball players that ever lived, and was on his way to a promising film director career before his life was cut tragically short. He is survived by his wife and three daughters, and was loved by many people.
